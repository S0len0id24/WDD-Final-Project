Causes Of Climate Change

By Khant Si Thu Hein

Burning fossil fuels like coal, oil, and natural gas releases large amounts of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere. This is the biggest human cause of climate change.

Deforestation removes trees that absorb CO₂. When forests are cut down, more heat-trapping gases stay in the atmosphere, increasing global warming.

Transportation is responsible for large CO₂ emissions. Cars, trucks, ships, and airplanes release greenhouse gases every day through fuel combustion.

Landfills produce methane as food waste and organic materials break down. Methane is a powerful greenhouse gas that contributes strongly to climate change.
